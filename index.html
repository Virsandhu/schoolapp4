Campus Life

school stadium

Established little before 1948

The school stadium has been used innumerable on state functions such as Republic/Independence day parade. YPS has given birth to many famous cricket players like Navjot Sidhu, Reetinder Sodhi, Dhruv Pandav(late).

school library

Established in 1948

The school has a large library with a stock of good collection of books on various subjects. The library subscribes to most of the national and local newspapers and magazines. Staff and children access and use the library on daily basis. To facilitate researching the vast content in the world wide web, a network of computers have been installed at the Library with high-speed internet connection.
